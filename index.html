quest for paws animal rescue and sanctuary

Pets change our lives. We're on a quest to change theirs

Quest for Paws Animal Rescue Sanctuary is a nonprofit organization dedicated to rescuing, fostering, and rehoming the abandoned, sick, and injured animals around our city.

With your help, we hope to make a difference in the lives of these wonderful animals

How we Help

Animal Rescue

We go on quests to rescue and recover any dog or cat roaming our streets.

Veternary Services

We provide free veternary treatment to strays, rescues, and pets.

Fostering Adoption

We help our rescues find their temporary and forever homes.

How You Can Help

There are countless ways you can help us in our quest for paws. You can make a donation (however big or small), join our fundraiser events, or volunteer your time and home as a foster parent.
